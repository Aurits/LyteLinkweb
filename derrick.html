About me?

Meet NEMWE DERRICK, a social media influencer with a passion for PUBLIC SPEAKING and INFLUENCING. Born and raised in JINJA, UGANDA, Derrick always had an eye for influencing and started his career as a twitter space host and blogger while studying at university. With his unique sense of content creation and relatable personality, Derrick quickly gained a following on twitter and Facebook. His engaging content includes business ads and IT related tutorials

As his following grew, Derrick began collaborating with various media houses and BUSINESS brands, including NILE BRIDGE COTTAGES HOTEL, ELPIS UGANDA, and MUTIMA VILLA APARTMENTS. His influence has also led to partnerships with numerous companies and hotels. Derrick's success as a social media influencer has earned him recognition in the industry. He has been invited to speak at conferences and events, sharing his insights on building a successful brand on social media. Beyond his work as an influencer, Derrick is also passionate about using his platform for good. He has partnered with organizations like ELPIS UGANDA that support mental health awareness , SHRH and body positivity, using his voice to promote messages of self-love and acceptance.

As he continues to inspire his followers with his content creation,Derrick remains committed to using his platform for positive change.
